About Me

Public Relations Born and raised in England, Michael Wallace aspired to build his career in London United Kingdom from the age of just 14.

By the time he was in his late 20s, after gaining experience in leading public relations agencies in both the UK and USA, Michael Wallace established his own agency, with an international outlook and portfolio of clients in the luxury lifestyle, property, travel and tourism sectors. Michael Wallace’s primary ambition was to challenge the industry standard of drenching editorial teams with client press releases, choosing instead to act strategically; taking time to build personal relationships with editors and journalists within the luxury travel, property and lifestyle market.

Michael Wallace now provides public relations representation and consultancy to privately-owned hotels and resorts, private islands, safari lodges and real estate developments across the globe and presently act as PRO for Baoyunju International Real estate and construction.
