    WHY CHOOSE US?

    Reasons Bao Yunju International Real Estate and Construction is the right contractor for your Real Estate or worldwide construction project . Everywhere in the world, construction is a complex and costly process that can challenge first-time and experienced owners equally. Whatever the scope or nature of the project, your success depends on choosing the right contractor. The right contractor offers a full package of experience, know-how and professionalism, backed by integrity, customer service and competitive pricing. It is of utmost importance that your contractor has a consistent track record of coming through for clients, no matter how big or small the project. In Real estate and construction, history makes a good case for the advantages of Bao Yunju International Real Estate and Construction .

    1. Experience

    With operations worldwide since 1995, we stand among the world's oldest general contractors, foreign or local. BaoYunju International Real Estate and Construction has successfully completed over 2000+ projects worldwide, more than 95% of active builders in China. Today, Bao Yunju International Real Estate and Construction has a broad geographic presence with jobs completed in major cities home and abroad.

    2. Know-How

    Only Bao Yunju International Real Estate and Construction combines the market knowledge and savvy of a local firm with the strength and resources of an international corporation. Our commitment to providing the international construction market with a brand new level of competence has lead us to assemble china's most impressive team of management, technical staff and field supervision. Beyond technical and organizational skills, Bao Yunju International Real Estate and Construction's project managers are also veteran navigators of china's daunting building-permit bureaucracies, a most essential part of the development or construction process.

    3. Professionalism

    When it comes to professionalism in International Real estate and construction,Bao Yunju International Real Estate and Construction is truly in a class of it’s own. Accurate and understandable bidding, scheduling, construction and legal documentation allow us to complete every job on time and on budget. It also keeps customers informed and involved. All of our jobs are approved with the local authorities in full compliance with Chinese law as well as international legislation .

    4. Integrity

    Bao Yunju International Real Estate and Construction has built its business and reputation by adhering to our core values of quality and dependability. Service and trust are at the heart of our relationships with our customers, and we develop, build, and nurture these relationships by demonstrating integrity, exercising sound judgment in our business and personal dealings, and living up to our commitments - doing what we say we will do.

    Historically, Bao Yunju International Real Estate and Construction has won over 80% of the tenders we have entered, much of the time by offering the lowest overall bid. Our policy is to profit from repeat business, responsible financial management and growth.

    We realize that the value of Bao Yunju International Real Estate and Construction is strictly defined by how much value we can offer our customers.
